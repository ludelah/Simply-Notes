notes.io

How to use:
  • Add: Click the "Add" button to add a note with the contents you put in
  • Delete: Click the X button to remove a note.
  • Archive: Click the ↓ button to archive the note, ↑ to unarchive.
  • Edit: Click inside a note to edit it's title and contents. Changes are saved when you click away.
  • Switch views: Use the "Show Archived Notes" button to view your archived notes.
